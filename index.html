About the CEO

Hello, my name is John Calderaio and I am the CEO and Founder of Online Gainesville, Alachua's premiere website and mobile application business. I graduated from the University of Florida with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering with Summa Cum Laude.

When I was 12, my grandpa handed down to me his old Power Macintosh while he upgraded to the brand new iMac at the time. From the moment I realized the joy I felt and the things I could create just by manipulating pixels on a screen, I was hooked.

If you are looking for a developer who loves what he does, handles business ethically, and has an obsessive attention to detail then please, reach out and send me a message. Have a great day!


- John Anthony Calderaio
